does creatine supplementation cause hair loss
Truey · Creatine & Hair Loss
Ollama · llama3.2

Current evidence does not strongly support a direct causal link between creatine supplementation and hair loss in most individuals. One small 2009 study found elevated DHT levels after creatine loading in rugby players, but no studies have directly measured hair follicle changes or clinical hair loss. The DHT hypothesis remains unconfirmed in larger or longer-term trials.
